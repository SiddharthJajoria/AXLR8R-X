Why Sponsor Us?

Axlr8r Formula Racing is a Formula SAE team of IIT, Delhi. The team comprises of around 25 undergraduate students from various departments who fabricate an open wheel electric formula race car. We innovate, design and excel! We wish to strengthen our endeavors to become a world-class team and participating at Formula Student Germany 2020 at Hockenheimring, Germany will take us one step closer to our goal. Your company being an industry major could help us with significant support and backing for a project of such a magnitude. It would be our privilege to have you as our sponsor. Your support would help us go a long way towards doing well at Formula Student Competition.
